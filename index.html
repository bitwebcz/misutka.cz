Mišutka - šití

Proč šít? Je to levnější? Spíš ne, možná trochu, kdyby člověk šil jen to, co by stejně kupoval.
Je výsledek lepší? Spíš ne, možná na druhej, třetí pokus (a musíte mít overlock).
Proč teda šít? Protože je to zábava a práce a to není málo aneb jak říkal Kurt Vonnegut:
“We are here on earth to fart around” . If this isn't nice, I don't know what is.

Margaret Atwood

I am certain that a Sewing Machine would relieve as much human suffering as a hundred Lunatic Asylums, and possibly a good deal more.

Oliver Wendell Holmes

Take your needle, my child, and work at your pattern; it will come out a rose by and by. Life is like that – one stitch at a time taken patiently and the pattern will come out all right like the embroidery.

Nathaniel Hawthorne

It is a token of healthy and gentle characteristics, when women of high thoughts and accomplishments love to sew; especially as they are never more at home with their hearts than while so occupied.
